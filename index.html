• Game Rules
    • Each player will take turns choosing their hand. At the end of each round, the winner recieves +1 point.
    • Winner is determined as follows:
    •   Rock crushes: Scissors & Lizard
    •   Paper covers: Rock & Spock
    •   Scissors cuts: Paper & Lizard
    •   Lizard eats: Spock & Paper
    •   Spock vaporizes: Scissors & Rock
